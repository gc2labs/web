Ollie

What is an Ollie?

Ollie is one of the easiest skateboardnig tricks. While making an ollie you and your board leap to the air without to use your hands.

Ollie is a fundamental skill in Skateboarding. It's often the first skill to be learned by a new skateboarder

How to do an Ollie?

To do an Ollie, you have to pop off the tail of your skateboard of the ground, while jumping and sliding your front foot forward
